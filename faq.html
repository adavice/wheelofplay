    How do I view a game?

    When you want to view a game at wheelofplay.com you need to log in. Sign in with the registered e-mail address and password in the top right corner. If you need help remembering your password, simply click on "Forgot password" and we will send you an e-mail. If you don’t have a user, you can quickly create a profile. You merely have to submit name, e-mail address and pick a password. It's quick and simple.     I don’t know my password. How do I reset it?

    If you signed up through one of our introductory offers, your password was automatically generated and sent to you by e-mail. If you created a membership on the wheelofplay.com, you picked your own password. In any case you can easily retrieve the password if you either forgot it or can't find the e-mail that was sent to you with login information. All you have to do is go to wheelofplay.com and click on "Log in" at the top right. You need to select "Forgot password" on the pop up and then enter the e-mail address that you signed up with. Once you have pressed confirm, you should receive your password in your inbox within a few minutes. If the e-mail fails to appear, please try checking your "spam folder" as they sometimes mistakenly end up there. Should the system report an error, try entering any other e-mail address you may have. Perhaps you accidentally signed up with an old e-mail address. If all fails, feel free to contact customer service.

    I have never signed up to this website. Why am I being charged?

The offer gives you a trial membership for a small charge, and often the opportunity to receive a welcome gift. To know more about your membership, please send an email to support@wheelofplay.com and we will send you the information to your email address.

    I am not receiving wheelofplay.com e-mails. How do I fix this?

    There may be several reasons as to why you are not receiving e-mails from wheelofplay.com. For instance, if you have an Outlook e-mail account (Hotmail, MSN, Live, etc.), you may experience problems. Check your spam filter to ensure that e-mails from wheelofplay.com are not accidentally ending up in this folder. Be sure to add the e-mail domain '@ wheelofplay.com' to your e-mail account's safe senders list. wheelofplay.com's automated e-mails are sent to you from support@wheelofplay.com. If you still are not receiving e-mails from wheelofplay.com, contact your ISP to find out whether your provider is blocking e-mail traffic from us. Please follow these guides to hopefully resolve the issue.

    Guide for Outlook:

    1. Sign in to your Outlook account.
    2. Click on the icon of 'Mail settings' in the upper right corner.
    3. Click on 'Options'.
    4. Find the section ‘Junk email’ and click on ‘Safe mailing lists'.
    5. Enter 'wheelofplay.com' in the box and click on the + icon to add.

    Guide for Outlook:

    1. Sign in to your Outlook account.
    2. Click on the icon of 'Mail settings' in the upper right corner.
    3. Click on 'Options'.
    4. Find the section ‘Junk email’ and click on ‘Safe mailing lists'.
    5. Enter 'wheelofplay.com' in the box and click on the + icon to add.

    Guide for Gmail:

    1. Log onto your Gmail account.
    2. Click on 'Settings' in the upper right-hand corner.
    3. Click on 'Filters'.
    4. Find 'Create a new filter' at the bottom of the page.
    5. Enter 'wheelofplay.com' in the 'From' field.
    6. Click on 'Next step'.
    7. Select 'Never send it to Spam'.
    8. Click on 'Create filter'.

    You should now be able to receive all e-mails sent from wheelofplay.com to your e-mail address. If you are still not receiving e-mails from us, please feel free to contact customer service.
